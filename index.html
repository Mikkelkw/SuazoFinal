What we do

Our Mission: The Suazo Business Center is a business resource committed to the development and empowerment of the Latino/Hispanic and other underserved communities. We provide assistance to help existing and potential minority entrepreneurs succeed and build wealth.
To achieve this goal, we offer business loans, training, and advising to our community of aspiring entrepreneurs. Together, we can help your business thrive.
