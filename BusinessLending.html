Business Lending

At Suazo, we understand that great ideas need a boost to become reality. Our consultants can help you access a loan, and figure out where it would be best allocated to help your business thrive. As stated in our mission, we cater mainly to the Latino/Hispanic and other underserved communities, but will gladly consider anybody for a loan.

The Suazo Center can help its students and clients obtain financing through traditional financial lending institutions, such as banks and credit unions, as well as supply loans internally. There is a lack of service for Utah's Hispanic/Latino and other minority populations when they want help starting their own businesses. We offer trainings, advising, workshops, and microloans to help people get started.


Since our inception, we have assisted in the creation of over 7,500 new companies in the community, resulting in tens of thousands of new jobs, and millions of dollars in added revenues to the region. We track and monitor these new businesses and follow their progress during their development processes.


To get started with the lending process, send an email to any of our Suazo team members, call at (801) 521-1709, or reach our front desk at frontdesk@suazocenter.org. We look forward to helping you finance and develop your business idea.
