Business Advising

Our business professionals are available by appointment to answer questions and help you develop your business plan. Refer to the Suazo Team page to get in touch with a specific adviser.

The Suazo Center provides personalized consultation with professional business advisors in order to accommodate the needs of its clients by answering questions regarding business registration, licensing, and implementation of concepts learned in class.


In order to provide students with practical experience, the programs will include various workshop-style classes that simulate the creation and management of a business. The workshop-style classes rotate to support students in each six-month stage. Refer to our Business Training page to learn more about group workshops.
